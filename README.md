# Iron Without a Crown: The Weight of the Banner

Iron Without a Crown: The Weight of the Banner is a long-form, narrative-driven 3D strategy RPG focused on leadership without authority, power without titles, and the long-term consequences of responsibility.

The player takes the role of a knight who refuses the crown and instead becomes the military and strategic commander of a fractured realm. Rather than ruling by decree, the player leads through decisions, loyalty, and sacrifice â€” shaping the future of the country over extended in-game years.

This project is designed as a **persistent world experience**. There is no definitive ending; the world continues to evolve based on the playerâ€™s actions, failures, and compromises.
